Frequently asked questions

Is Faceblock really private?

Yes. Faceblock runs 100% in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server. All face detection and image processing happens locally on your device using WebGL. You can even turn on airplane mode and it will still work — no internet connection needed.

What face anonymization effects are available?

Faceblock offers 10 effects: blur, pixelate, eye bar, blackout, emoji, solid color, glitch, swirl, silhouette, and redact. Each effect has adjustable intensity.

Can I blur faces in multiple photos at once?

Yes. Faceblock supports batch processing of up to 20 photos at once. Faces are auto-detected across all images, and you can download them individually or as a ZIP file.

Does Faceblock work on mobile?

Yes. Faceblock is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. It supports touch gestures, paste from clipboard, and camera capture. You can also install it as an app on your home screen for quick access, but there's no need to — it works just as well straight from the browser.

Is Faceblock free to use?

Yes, Faceblock is completely free with no sign-up required. It is open source and has no ads, no tracking, and no usage limits.
